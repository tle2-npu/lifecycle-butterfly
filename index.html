Metamorphosis

Metamorphosis is a Greek word that means transformation or change in shape

Insects have two common types of metamorphosis:

Complete metamorphosis

The young (called a larva instead of a nymph) is very different from the adults. It also usually eats different types of food

Such as moths and flies

Incomplete metamorphosis

The young (called a nymph) usually look like small adults but without the wings

Such as grasshoppers and crickets
